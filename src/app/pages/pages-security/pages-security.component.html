Security Features

1.

End-to-end Encryption with Open Source Cryptography

Whether you’re sending classified information to your accountant, business partners, clients, news networks or simply your friends, your emails are automatically encrypted using strong public key cryptography on our Icelandic servers, so even we can’t read them. Only you and your recipient can decode your emails.

2.

Benefit from the World’s Strictest Data Protection Laws

Most encrypted email service providers boast about their Swiss data centers. What they fail to tell you is that the Swiss government has an information sharing agreement with other countries, meaning your data could be shared with others without your consent. Iceland has zero sharing agreements and maintains the strictest data privacy laws in the world, so your data stays encrypted on our servers and nowhere else.

3.

Guaranteed Anonymity and Zero Access to Your Data

We do not require any personal information to create your encrypted email account. Simply chose a username and password to access your email interface. Our industry leading “Hash & Salt” technique guarantee even we do not know your login information. For additional security, we do not store your IP logs, which can be traced to your anonymous email account. Your privacy is guaranteed with CTemplar.

4.

Simple-to-use Interface Just Like a Regular Email Account

Unlike the old days where encrypted emailing meant complex manual encryption CTemplar does it all for you. Simply send and receive your emails, just like a regular email account, and rest assured knowing your privacy is 100% secure.
