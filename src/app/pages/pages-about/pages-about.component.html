CTemplar’s Legal Stance

CTemplar’s Legal Stance

CTemplar’s legal team views internet privacy as a dynamic issue. It is an evolving landscape. Accordingly, static solutions will not afford your data the level of protection demanded by those who value security.
We have researched all aspects of internet privacy in various jurisdictions in order to assure that our servers are domiciled in the most secure location. Our servers are located in Iceland, recognized as the premier location for internet privacy. The Icelandic government has demonstrated itself to be at the forefront of internet privacy and vigorously enforces the right to be free of government intrusion.
Our legal department continues to research internet security and privacy laws throughout the world so that we may constantly provide users with the maximum level of internet privacy. If there is a proposed change in policy, we will be first to know and have a migration plan in place that ensures complete ongoing security.
