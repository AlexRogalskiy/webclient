Additional Security Benefits With Tor

Tor is free software for enabling anonymous communication. Tor directs Internet traffic through a free, worldwide, volunteer overlay network consisting of more than seven thousand relays to conceal a user's location and usage from anyone conducting network surveillance or traffic analysis.

You must download Tor to be able to use it. You can download it here: https://www.torproject.org/projects/torbrowser.html.en
