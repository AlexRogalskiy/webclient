Security features

1.
Ready to regain your right to privacy?

Whether you're sending classified information to your accountant, business, partners, clients, news networks or simply your friends, your emails are automatically encrypted using strong public key cryptography on our Iceland-based servers. Only you and your recipient can decode your emails; we can't even read them.

2.
Benefit from the world's strictest data protection laws

Most encrypted email service providers boast about their Swiss data centers. What they don't tell you is the Swiss government has an information sharing agreement with the United States, so your data isn't 100% secure. Iceland has no sharing agreements with the U.S. and maintains the strictest data privacy laws in the world, so your data stays encrypted on our servers and nowhere else.

3.
100% anonymity and ZERO access to your data

We don't require any personal information to create your encrypted email account. Simply choose a username and password to set up and access your email interface. For added security, we don't store your IP logs, which can be traced to your anonymous email account. With CTemplar, your privacy is guaranteed.

4.
Simple-to-use interface just like a regular email account

Unlike the old days where encrypted emailing meant complex manual encryption and private server setup, CTemplar does it all for you. Send and receive your emails just like a regular email account and rest assured knowing your privacy is 100% maintained.

5.
Encrypted email to non-CTemplar emails, self-destructing messages & more

Your contacts don't require a CTemplar account in order to send and receive encrypted email. However, we recommend everyone use CTemplar email accounts for maximum security since your stored emails and subject lines will be protected, unlike regular accounts.
